Introduction

I serve up a curated list of songs weekly that will satisfy your appetite

I have loved music my whole life but when I started college I started to find I had a passion for electronica.

Specifically house music is that perfect middle ground between the heavy noise of dubstep and the continuous melody of trance.

I am no longer part of the club and rave scene but still enjoy the music and continue to appreciate its evolution over time.
